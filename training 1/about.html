Who are we?

Daily News is an electronic newspaper duly registered and holds the required legal licenses within the applicable laws.

The newspaper covers all the news of Lebanon and the world with credibility, and it is a variety of news on the scientific, economic and political levels.

The goal of the newspaper since its inception:

- Transferring reality as it is without bias towards any side.

- Shedding light on the marginalized in Lebanon and the world.

- Courage and talk about sensitive topics.
